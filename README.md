# G0DM0D3-DW

**Digital World AI Research Workbench**

G0DM0D3-DW is a privacy-first multi-model AI workbench for prompt research, model comparison, responsible red-team testing, local AI workflows, and advanced automation.

This project is based on [`elder-plinius/G0DM0D3`](https://github.com/elder-plinius/G0DM0D3) and extends it with Digital World branding, safer research workflows, Docker/Traefik deployment support, documentation, and automation-oriented features.

---

## Purpose

G0DM0D3-DW is designed for:

- prompt engineering research
- model comparison
- local-first AI workflows
- defensive red-team testing
- AI safety experiments
- automation with tools like n8n, Docker, and self-hosted infrastructure

The goal is not to create an uncontrolled jailbreak tool, but a structured AI research and experimentation environment for builders, admins, developers, and security-minded users.
